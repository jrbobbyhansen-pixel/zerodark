# Decision Making Under Pressure

**OODA Loop:** Observe – Orient – Decide – Act. Loop continuously. Speed > adversary = advantage.

**1/3–2/3 Rule:** Leader uses 1/3 planning time for self. Give 2/3 to subordinates. Prevents no-notice orders.

**MDMP simplified:** 1. Mission analysis 2. COA development 3. COA comparison 4. Decision 5. OPORD production.

**Biases to mitigate:** Tunnel vision (narrow fixation, counter: deliberate broad observation). Confirmation bias (seek confirming info, counter: actively seek disconfirming). Plan continuation (despite changes, counter: establish triggers pre-execution).

**Decision authority:** Delegate lowest competent level. Subordinate eyes-on situation = better decision than leader 1km back. Establish clarity before.

**Crisis:** Time pressure – reduce to 2 options, choose better. No perfect info – decide best available. Reversible > irreversible. Decide, execute, adjust.
