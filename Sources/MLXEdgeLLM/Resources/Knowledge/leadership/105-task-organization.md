# Task Organization

**Definition:** Assign specific tasks and resources to elements for specific operation.

**Purpose:** Right capabilities to right tasks. Prevent overload. Clear authority/responsibility.

**Cross-attachment:** Other unit elements to your unit for operation. Provides operational control – attached follows orders this operation.

**Task structure:** For each: assigned element (who), task (what), purpose (why), time (when), constraint (how). "Team ALPHA: establish OP grid 14SNA064285 NLT 0300 18MAR provide early warning enemy approach Route 7."

**Relationships:** Direct support (dedicated specific). General support (entire force). Reinforcing (add capability).

**Communication:** Brief mission. Post task organization diagram. Each subordinate knows: their job, others' jobs, how elements support.

**Flexibility:** Changes during operation. Authority change execution. Commander attach/detach/reassign. Subordinates ready receive tasks.
