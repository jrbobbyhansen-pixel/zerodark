# Leadership Under Stress

**Effects:** Gross motor – IMPROVE (adrenaline). Fine motor – DETERIORATE. Cognitive – narrows (tunnel vision), quality degrades extreme. Communication – volume up, clarity down. Use deliberate speech.

**Team cohesion:** Physical presence – leader where action is. Visible = stabilizing. Calm voice – appears calm (even if not) stabilizes. Action focus – give tasks. Idle personnel spiral. Action breaks cycle. Information – tell situation known – uncertainty worse than bad news.

**Stress casualties:** Inappropriate laughter/crying. Can't perform trained tasks. Freezing. Repetitive behavior (shaking, rocking). Aggression without target. Isolate, calm, simple task.

**Personal management:** Box breathing – 4 in, 4 hold, 4 out, 4 hold. Reduces cortisol. Tunnel vision counter – pause, look away, observe 5 sec. Decision anchoring – return training, what did training say?

**Exhaustion and decisions:** 18+ hours awake = drunk equivalent. Rotate decision makers. Sleep mandatory sustained ops. Assign sleep time operational requirement.
