# After Action Review (AAR)

**Purpose:** Identify what happened, why, what to sustain/improve. Not blame. Honest analysis builds competence.

**1. Planned task and intent?** State original mission, commander's intent, expected outcomes.

**2. What actually occurred?** Chronological factual account. Use timeline.

**3. Worked well and why?** Specific actions/decisions success. Identify reason – replicate.

**4. Should improve and how?** Specific failures/near-failures. Root cause. Specific corrective action.

**5. Differ next time?** Specific executable changes – training, tactics, equipment, planning.

**Rules:** All ranks equal. No rank-shielding. Actions not personalities. Everyone speaks – junior often best observations.

**Documentation:** Written distributed. Lessons in SOP. Follow-up training assigned.

**Timing:** Hot – immediately (fresh memory). Cold – after analysis (additional info).
