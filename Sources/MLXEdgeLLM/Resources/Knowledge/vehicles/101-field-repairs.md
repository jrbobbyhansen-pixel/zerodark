# Field Mechanical Repairs

**Flat tire:** Loosen lugs before jacking. Jack at point. Remove nuts, flat. Install spare. Hand-tighten star pattern. Lower. Torque to spec. Spare limits: 50mph, 50 miles max.

**Overheating:** AC off, heater on (draws heat). Red gauge – pull over immediately. NO open radiator cap hot (steam). Cool 30 min. Check coolant. Low – add water emergency (not permanent). Blown hose – duct tape, zip ties temp. Blown gasket – white smoke, coolant loss, limited distance possible.

**Battery:** Jump – red dead positive, red good positive, black good negative, black dead ground. Start good vehicle, start dead, remove reverse. Terminals – clean baking soda + water, tighten.

**Fuel:** Running out – coast 20–50 miles after light. Contamination (water diesel) – hard start, rough idle, drain tank. Clogged filter – power loss, rough idle, replace.

**Electrical:** Blown fuse – locate, identify (visibly melted), replace same amperage. Repeatedly blows = short circuit, fix cause.
