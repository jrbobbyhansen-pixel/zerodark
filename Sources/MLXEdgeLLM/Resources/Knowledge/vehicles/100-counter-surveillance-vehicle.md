# Counter-Surveillance – Vehicle

**Detection:** Three sightings same vehicle different locations = surveillance confirmed. Two = suspected, begin SDR.

**Indicators:** Same vehicle same turns. Consistent following distance. Multiple occupants suspicious. Appears, disappears, reappears.

**SDR route:** Choke points force commit. U-turns, one-ways, parking structure entry/exit. Vary speed unpredictable. Unnecessary stops.

**Losing surveillance:** One-way quick turn. Parking structure (enter, pause, alternate exit). Dense urban tight turns. Highway (specific speed clear traffic, speed pull away).

**Counter-drone vehicle:** High-sided (trucks, vans) reduce aerial angle. Covered (bridges, tunnels, structures) deny observation momentarily. Change vehicle/direction after cover.

**What they look for:** Counter-surveillance behavior (mirror checks, irregular speeds, SDR). Consistent passenger. Consistent vehicle. Predictable routes/times.

**Electronic:** GPS tracker – RF detector sweep before sensitive movement. Locations: wheel wells, under bumpers, chassis behind exhaust, interior for cellular.
