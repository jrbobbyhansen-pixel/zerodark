# Vehicle Security

**Pre-departure:** Walk around before every movement. Tampering signs (antenna, wires, devices), low tire, doors/hood not latched, anomalies.

**Parking:** Front-out (faster departure). Multiple escapes. Avoid isolated night. Note surroundings – anomalies = possible tampering.

**Anti-theft:** Kill switch (fuel pump/ignition). Wheel lock. Clamp. Delays casual theft only – determined adversary needs minutes.

**Driver awareness:** Vary routes (no pattern). Vary times. Note following vehicles (multiple turns).

**Communications:** Radio contact moving. Check-in times. Miss = initiate response. Brief passengers on plan.

**Overnight security:** Fuel locked/drained (long-term). Departure position (never boxed). Area security established.

**Under surveillance:** Drive to safe place (police station, public area). Don't lead to safe house. Note: make, model, registration, occupants.
