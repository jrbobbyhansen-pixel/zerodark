# Vehicle Recovery – Stuck and Disabled

**Mud/sand:** Reduce tire pressure 5–10 PSI (footprint, traction). Rock vehicle forward-reverse. Don't spin (digs deeper). Dig around tires. Add traction (branches, mats, clothing, sand ladder).

**Winching:** Anchor – tree strap, 8in+ diameter. Double-line pull (2x pulling capacity) – snatch block to anchor, cable through and back. Pull straight winching direction (side loads damage).

**Tow straps:** Dynamic (kinetic energy recovery) stretches, uses vehicle energy. NEVER chain or rigid (jerk damages frame).

**Hi-lift jack:** Winch (pin bumper/anchor, ratchet extract). Spreader (lift side, add traction material). Dangerous – vehicle chocked, base plate soft ground.

**Disabled crew:** Security 360° immediately. Comms. Recovery call. If fire – extract personnel, leave vehicle. If safe – assess damage (flat, mechanical), attempt field repair.

**Tire repair:** Plug kit – insert, reinflate. Plug + patch – superior. After repair 50mph max. Run flat – 50 miles max reduced speed.
