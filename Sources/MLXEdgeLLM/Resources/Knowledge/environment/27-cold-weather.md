# Cold Weather Operations

## Wind Chill

32°F + 30mph = 15°F effective. 0°F + 30mph = -26°F effective (exposed skin freezes in 30 minutes).

## Clothing System (COLD Principle)

- Keep it **C**lean (dirty insulation loses effectiveness)
- Avoid **O**verheating (sweat = wet = dead)
- Wear **L**oose and in **L**ayers (adjustable, traps air)
- Keep it **D**ry (cotton kills — loses all insulative value when wet, use wool or synthetic)

## Frostbite

White/gray/waxy/hard tissue. Do NOT rewarm if refreezing risk. Rewarm in 100-104°F water 15-30 minutes (pain = nerves recovering = good). Do NOT rub.

## Movement Produces Heat

But generates sweat. Vent clothing during exertion to prevent soaking base layers.

## Night Shelter

Quinzhee or snow trench. Sleeping platform elevated above entrance (cold air sinks). Ventilation hole with stick.
