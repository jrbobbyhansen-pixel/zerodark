# Jungle Operations

**Disease priority:** Malaria, dengue, typhoid, dysentery, leptospirosis. Countermeasures: DEET 30%+, long sleeves/pants, bed net, water purification, foot hygiene.

**Water:** All contaminated. Clear streams: Giardia, bacteria, virus. Standing: plus parasites. Purify all. Sources: bamboo (knock, listen for water), vine (cut high, collect bottom – bitter = unsafe), banana plant (cut, hollow, collect seep).

**Movement:** Slash at angle. Follow water downstream. 200–300m/hr max dense jungle. Maintain bearing (jungle disorienting).

**Shelter:** Elevated (insects, flood, snakes, moisture). Hammock preferred. Platform: 4 posts, cross poles, bark floor 3ft.

**Food:** Insects high-protein. Palm heart (cut coconut base, eat inner raw). Tropical fruits if tested. Avoid: bright frogs (toxic skin), unknown mushrooms.

**Foot care:** Primary failure point. Change socks 2x daily. Air feet. Dry. Jungle rot (immersion foot) 24–48 hrs wet. Treat early: dry, antifungal, rest.
