# Arctic and Cold Weather Operations

**Prevention:** COLD principle: Clean insulation (dirty loses), Overheating avoidance (sweat = death), Loose and Layered (adjustable), Dry (cotton kills).

**Clothing:** Base – synthetic/wool wicking. NEVER cotton (retains moisture, loses insulation). Mid – down (best warmth/weight) or synthetic. Shell – wind/water proof, breathable. Gloves: liner + outer. Boots: rated below expected temp.

**Water:** Melt snow (don't eat – lowers core temp). Yellow snow = contaminated. Heat, not body heat (burns calories).

**Fire:** Elevated platform (prevents sinking). Dry wood (conifer under snow). Pine pitch excellent starter in cold.

**Navigation:** Whiteout – compass only. Rope-linked. Stop if worsens. Sun circle: low trajectory. Lowest = north (northern hemisphere).

**Frostbite:** Prevention > treatment. Check extremities every 30 min. Buddy checks ears, nose, cheeks. Warm in armpits/groin (core warm).

**Avalanche:** Convex slopes risky (tension fractures). Fresh snow + wind = loading. If caught: swim stay on surface. Buried: air pocket, spit find up, excavate.
