# Mountain and High Altitude Operations

**Altitude sickness:** AMS (headache, nausea, fatigue >8000ft). Treat: stop, rest, descend if no improve. HACE (ataxia, confusion, hallucination) – DESCEND IMMEDIATELY. HAPE (cough, pink sputum, breathing at rest) – DESCEND IMMEDIATELY.

**Acclimatization:** Max 1000ft/day >10000ft. Rest day every 3rd. "Climb high, sleep low."

**Dehydration:** Respiratory loss increases (cold dry air). Urine increases. Hydrate 2x normal. 12000ft: 4–6L/day.

**Terrain:** Ridgelines exposed – descend below treeline if storm. Scree – move diagonal, test. Talus – test stability. Snow – probe crevasses.

**Lightning:** >treeline – descend. If caught: don't shelter under lone trees. Crouch on balls, insulation, 50ft apart. Avoid highest ground.

**Navigation:** Contours critical. Ridgeline handrail. Water catching feature. Map terrain frequently – clouds reduce visibility suddenly.
