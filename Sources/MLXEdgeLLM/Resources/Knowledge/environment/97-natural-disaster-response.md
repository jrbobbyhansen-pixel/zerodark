# Natural Disaster Response

**Earthquake:** During – DROP, COVER, HOLD. Hands/knees, under table/wall, protect head. Away from windows, exterior, heavy objects. NO run outside. After – check injuries, damage, gas (smell). No open flame if gas. Expect aftershocks. Move unstable structures.

**Flood:** Never walk >knee-deep moving water (2ft sweeps vehicle). Move high ground immediately. Vehicle stalled – exit if rising. Trapped – roof safer. After – water contamination, purify all, avoid floodwater (sewage, chemicals, pathogens).

**Wildfire:** Move perpendicular to fire (uphill/wind = fastest spread). Cover all skin (radiant burns). Don't shelter in structure (burns, thermal effects). If caught – cleared area, lie flat face down, cover, breathe ground level.

**Tornado:** Immediate – basement/lowest interior room, center, away from windows. Last resort – ditch/culvert below grade. NO mobile home, NO overpass (wind tunnel), NO perpendicular driving.

**Hurricane:** Evacuate if directed. If not – interior rooms, lowest floor above flood. Stay through eye (second half equally severe). NO drive through flooded roads.
