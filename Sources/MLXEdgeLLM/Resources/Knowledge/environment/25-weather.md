# Field Weather Prediction

## Pressure Indicators

Smoke rises straight = high pressure/stable. Smoke drifts low = low pressure/unstable. Old injury pain increasing = pressure dropping.

## Clouds

- **Cirrus** (high thin wispy) = fair now, weather in 24-48 hours
- **Cumulus** (flat-bottom puffy) = fair weather
- **Cumulonimbus** (anvil top, dark base) = thunderstorm within 30 minutes to 2 hours
- **Altocumulus** (mackerel sky) = change within 12-24 hours

## Wind

Steadily increasing wind = approaching front. Wind shifting to north/northeast in northern hemisphere = storm approach.

## "Red Sky at Night, Sailor's Delight; Red Sky in Morning, Sailor's Warning"

Valid meteorological principle.

## Lightning

Count seconds between flash and thunder, divide by 5 = distance in miles. Seek cover under 6 miles (30 seconds). **30-30 rule**: shelter when under 30 seconds.
