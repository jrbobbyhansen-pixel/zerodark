# Desert Operations

## Water Requirement

1 gallon per person per day minimum. More with activity. Survival time without water in extreme desert = hours, not days.

## Finding Water

Base of cliff faces (seepage). Dry washes, dig 1-3ft at outer bends. Dense vegetation (cottonwood/willow/mesquite trace water). Animal and bird trails converge at water at dawn and dusk.

## Travel

Move only at dawn/dusk/night. Rest in shade 1000-1600.

## Signaling

Mirror or reflective surface visible 10+ miles in clear desert air.

## Flash Floods

Never camp in dry drainages. Wall of water arrives without visible rain. Warning signs: roaring sound upstream, debris in normally dry wash.
