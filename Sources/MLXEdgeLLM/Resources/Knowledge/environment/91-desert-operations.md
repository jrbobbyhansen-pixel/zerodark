# Desert Operations

**Heat priority:** Death from heat: hours without water + activity. Minimize heat, maximize shade.

**Schedule:** 0400–0900 max move. 0900–1600 minimal, full shade. 1600–2000 moderate. 2000–0400 night (dark, cool).

**Water:** 1 gal/day min rest. 2 gal activity. 3 gal heavy. Plan 25% extra.

**Finding:** Dry wash intersections (dig 1–3ft). Cliff bases (seeps). Vegetation (cottonwood/willow/mesquite trace). Bird/animal trails (dawn/dusk). Dew (early morning).

**Shade:** Ground 150°F. Air 30in up 20–30°F cooler. Shade reduces 20–40°F. Even partial (rock, vehicle, tarp).

**Prevention:** Acclimatization 10–14 days. Buddy system. Urine monitoring (dark = dehydrated).

**Flash flood:** Never camp in wash. If can't see 5+ miles upstream, don't shelter in drainage. Warning: distant thunder, debris, roaring.
