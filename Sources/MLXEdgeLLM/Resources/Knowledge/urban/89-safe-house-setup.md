# Safe House – Selection and Setup

**Criteria:** Multiple entry/exit (2-3 min). Not on main street. Water access. Previous occupants unknown. Structural integrity confirmed. Comms range.

**Assessment:** Enter/exit via primary and alternates. No single chokepoint. Verify utilities. Check previous occupation (surveillance, competing interest).

**Setup:** Cover windows (can't see light outside). Observation of approaches (rotate watcher). Mark emergency exits.

**Counter-surveillance:** Never all personnel simultaneously. Stagger arrivals/departures 30+ min. No direct route. Use SDR.

**Communications:** Check-in times. Miss one = heightened concern. Miss two = compromised, evacuate. Alternate rally point known to all.

**Sanitation:** Minimize trash. No disposal obvious. Waste plan from day one. Food + 2-day contingency.

**Departure:** Leave no sign. Remove all waste, materials, sign. Sanitize comms (destroy written). Alternate exit route.
