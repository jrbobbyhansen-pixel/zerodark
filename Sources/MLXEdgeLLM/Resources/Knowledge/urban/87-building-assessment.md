# Building Assessment – Structural Safety

**Exterior check:** Foundation cracks (horizontal = failure, diagonal = stress). Leaning wall = compromised. Fire damage – beams may look intact but lost capacity.

**Interior:** Floors – bounce test (flex = compromised). Ceiling – bulging, staining = water damage. Stairs – test each step.

**Building types:** Wood – burns, good concealment, poor cover. Masonry – good cover, variable stability. Concrete – excellent cover, stable. Steel – good cover, predictable collapse in fire.

**Tactical value:** Observation (windows/roof views). Cover (wall thickness, materials). Concealment (entry visibility). Entry/exit (minimum 2). Defensibility.

**Hazmat:** Industrial (chemicals, gases). Hospitals (meds, radiation). Labs (unknown chemicals). Broken containers = assume hazardous, ventilate.

**Utilities:** Gas – shut off if smell. Electrical – assume live. Water – shut off if contaminated.
