# Urban Water and Food Sourcing

**Water in abandoned urban:** Hot water heater tanks (40–80 gal potable). Toilet tanks (3–5 gal). Water pipes retain pressure 24–48hr. Ice in freezers. Canned goods liquid.

**Water quality:** Municipal water safe weeks in pipes. Standing water contaminated in days. Purify all water.

**Food sourcing:** Commercial buildings (restaurants, warehouses) – preserved goods last years. Frozen – usable days/weeks. Residential pantries – 3–7 days. Rooftop gardens. Urban wildlife (cook thoroughly).

**Contamination risks:** Broken mains draw sewage. Floodwater contaminates. Gas leaks, chemicals, fire suppression contaminate. Assess each source.
