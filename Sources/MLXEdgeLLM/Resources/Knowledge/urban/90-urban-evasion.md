# Urban Evasion

**Blend before running:** If surveillance unconfirmed, walk naturally, enter building and observe.

**Surveillance detection:** SDR through chokepoints (alleys, stairs) forces exposure. Three confirmed sightings = surveillance. Two = suspected.

**Losing foot:** Enter building with multiple exits (mall, hotel, parking). Change appearance (coat, hat, bag). Exit different direction. Underground transport (subway). Crowded venue.

**Losing vehicle:** Unpredictable direction. U-turns. One-ways. Parking structure (enter, pause, alternate exit).

**Under surveillance:** Don't lead to safe house. Move to sterile location. Contact support. Plan: meet at dead drop, get instructions.

**Electronic evasion:** Cell phone in Faraday or disabled. Avoid patterns (same place, route). Change appearance before sensitive movement.

**Active pursuit:** Increase pace (surveillance must match, becomes obvious). Crowded venue (aggressor hesitant). Seek uniformed presence (deters). Pre-identified safe haven.
