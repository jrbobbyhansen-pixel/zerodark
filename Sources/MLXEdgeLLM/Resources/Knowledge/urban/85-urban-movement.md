# Urban Movement

Route selection: avoid main streets (canalization risk). Use parallel side streets. Map route: cover positions, chokepoints, alternates, emergencies.

Movement techniques: stay close to buildings. Cross intersections at right angles quickly. Never silhouette in doorways. Pie corners: gain angle incrementally.

Corner clearance: slow pie – approach offset 2–3ft. Step lateral incrementally gaining angle. See threat before exposing yourself.

Danger areas: intersections – cross individually at corners, fast, perpendicular to fire. Open areas – never without observation and overwatch.

Building-to-building: upper floors preferred for observation. Ground level for structural stability. Move at night if possible.

Vehicle movement: vary routes. Don't slow unnecessarily. Know alternates. Don't hang out windows. Avoid narrow streets that prevent U-turns.

Terrain compartmentalization: urban divides into compartments (blocks). Clear each before moving. Never skip compartments.
