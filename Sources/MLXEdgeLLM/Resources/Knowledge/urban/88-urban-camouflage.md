# Urban Camouflage and Concealment

**Blend:** Dress matches population. Avoid military appearance. Gait and behavior: purpose, not urgency. Appear to belong.

**Window discipline:** Never silhouette. Observe from inside shadow, 6+ ft away. Light discipline: no light visible outside. Cover windows before artificial light.

**Entry/exit:** Don't use primary entrance under observation. Find alternates. Vary times.

**Signature:** No food smells (cooking attracts). No smoke, light, unusual noise. No garbage accumulation.

**Vehicle:** Park in structures. Remove from line-of-sight. Cover if in open. Park alongside similar vehicles.

**Rooftop:** Excellent observation, terrible concealment. Thermal visible from air. If used: back from edge, overhead cover, limit time.

**Pattern avoidance:** Vary times, entrances, activities. Rotate observation positions.
