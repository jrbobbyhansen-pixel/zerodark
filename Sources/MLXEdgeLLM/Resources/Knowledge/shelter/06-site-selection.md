# Shelter Site Selection

## Avoid

- Hilltops (wind, lightning)
- Valley bottoms (cold air drainage, flooding, insects)
- Single tall trees (lightning)
- Dry riverbeds (flash flooding)
- Animal trails and dens

## Look For

- Natural windbreaks (ridge sides, dense thickets, terrain masking)
- Drainage away from site
- Materials close by (debris, branches)
- 100-200ft above valley floor (out of cold air drainage, protected from wind)

## Check Overhead

Dead branches before building under any tree (widow makers).

## Tactical Site (BLISS)

Blend in – Low silhouette – Irregular shape – Small – Secluded. Two withdrawal routes minimum.
