# Hunting and Trapping

## Figure-4 Deadfall

Three sticks carved with interlocking notches. Heavy flat rock balanced on trigger. Animal disturbs bait stick – rock falls. Effective for squirrel, rabbit, groundhog. Set on game trails, near burrows.

## Wire Snare

28-gauge stiff wire. Loop diameter slightly larger than animal's head (fist for rabbit, golf ball for squirrel). Set at nose height (3-4in off ground for rabbit). Placed on active trail confirmed by tracks and scat. Anchor to stake or bent sapling.

## Spear

Straight hardwood 6ft. Sharpen point. Fire-harden (rotate in dry heat above fire, not in flames).

## Bait

Peanut butter, berries, seeds. Place at snare not on snare.

## Processing

Gut first (cut anus to sternum, remove organs). Keep heart and liver (high nutrition). Skin by cutting around legs and pulling peel off. Boil or cook until no pink – all wild game to 165°F internal.

## Gut Pile

Remove 50ft from camp. Attracts predators.
