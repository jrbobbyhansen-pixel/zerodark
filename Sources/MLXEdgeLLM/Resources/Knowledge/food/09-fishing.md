# Field Fishing

## Improvised Hook

Bent wire, safety pin, carved bone with barb.

## Line

Paracord inner strands, sinew, strong plant fiber.

## Bait

Earthworms (under rocks in moist soil), insects, grubs, bright cloth scraps.

## Spear Fishing

Harden point in fire. Wade slowly. Aim BELOW fish (refraction makes them appear higher than actual position).

## Fish Trap

Weave branches into cone with narrow entrance pointing inward. Place in stream with current flow. Fish swim in for bait, cannot find exit.

## All Fish

Cook thoroughly. Remove gills and gut before cooking.
