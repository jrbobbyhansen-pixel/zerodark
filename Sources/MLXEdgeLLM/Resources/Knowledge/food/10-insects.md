# Insects as Emergency Food

Insects provide 6-8g protein per 100g. Most are safe when cooked.

## Avoid

Brightly colored insects (warning coloration), hairy caterpillars, stinging insects.

## Safe Targets

Crickets, grasshoppers (remove legs/wings), ants (formic acid cooks off), termites, beetle grubs (white C-shaped larvae in rotting wood), earthworms.

## Collection

Crickets under rocks at night. Ants from mounds. Grubs in rotting logs.

## Preparation

Roast or boil kills parasites. Remove hard parts (wings, legs). Mash into paste if needed. Nutritional value: grasshopper rivals beef protein per gram.
