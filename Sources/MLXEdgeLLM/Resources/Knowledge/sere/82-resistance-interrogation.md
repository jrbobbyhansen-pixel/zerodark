# Resistance to Interrogation

**Legal obligations:** Geneva Convention: provide Name, Rank, Service number, Date of birth. Nothing more required. Under torture or duress: provide only Big Four regardless of pressure.

**Techniques to recognize:** Rapport building. False promises. Good cop/bad cop. False evidence. Isolation. Sensory deprivation. Sleep deprivation.

**Resistance principles:** Provide Big Four only. Every piece of info is intelligence. Resist operational information regardless of method. Don't speculate or discuss operations "hypothetically." Maintain cover story.

**Maintaining composure:** Mental anchoring: hold identity and values. Remember purpose. Controlled breathing: 4-4-4 box breathing reduces cortisol. Mental compartmentalization: focus immediate moment.
