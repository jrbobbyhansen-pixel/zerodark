# Evasion Planning – Escape and Evasion Routes

Establish E&E route before every mission. All team members brief on route. Establish rally points (primary, alternate). ISOPREP completed: personal ID data, authentication, blood type, medical conditions.

**Rally points:** Near: 100–300m. Far: 2–5km. Time at RP: 30 min max. If teammates don't arrive, continue E&E individually.

**E&E route selection:** Terrain masking, water routes (disrupt tracking), avoid roads (checkpoints). Move toward friendly territory or water. Identify safe houses, friendly contacts.

**Bona fides:** Signals to identify self to rescuers. Pre-established (code words, physical signals) known to both sides. Never reveal unless positive friendly identity confirmed.

**Authentication:** Personal codes from ISOPREP. Challenge/response known only to you and command. Confirm rescue aircraft or forces are friendly.

**Cache planning:** Pre-position emergency cache: water, food, first aid, signaling, clothing. Mark relative to terrain features, not GPS.
