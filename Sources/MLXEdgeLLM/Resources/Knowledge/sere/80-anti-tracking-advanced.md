# Advanced Anti-Tracking

**Ground sign:** Move on hard surfaces (rock, concrete, packed dirt). Step on individual rocks. Move in existing footstep depressions. Wrap feet in cloth.

**Stream use:** Enter from bank, walk 200m+ upstream/downstream, exit hard bank. Stream breaks scent and footprint trails. Exit away from intended route.

**False trail:** Lay deliberate sign in wrong direction. Beat ground, leave obvious tracks, vegetation toward false destination. Backtrack on same footprints. Diverge on hard ground.

**Scent denial:** Wild animal urine masks scent. Crushed plants on boots and clothing reduce but don't eliminate scent. Avoid strong odors (food, soap, tobacco, fuel).

**Timing:** Night: reduces visual detection, thermal signature, aerial observation. Dawn: dew muffles sound, fresh vegetation reduces scent. Rain: washes sign, masks sound, grounds scent.

**Observer counter-detection:** Before entering area, observe 5–10 min from concealment. Back-check trail at 50m intervals. Observe potential surveillance positions. Irregular pace and direction changes defeat pattern detection.
