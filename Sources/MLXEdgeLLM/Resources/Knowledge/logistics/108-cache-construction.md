# Cache – Construction and Recovery

**Purpose:** Pre-position supplies for future. Reduce carry weight, supply points, extended ops.

**Site:** Natural concealment (overhang, tree hollow, roots). Not trail. Durable terrain feature (lasts without GPS). Dry. Off primary movement routes.

**Container:** Waterproof (ammo can, vacuum bags, military container). Line absorbent (silica gel inside). Consider extremes, moisture, animals (food smells).

**Packing:** Individual waterproof bags. Cushion. Include inventory, date, expiration dates.

**Location:** Relative terrain features visible without GPS: "Base dead pine double trunk, 12 paces east stream crossing, left bank downstream." Not: GPS (captured, batteries die). Confirm bearing/distance compass before burial.

**Recovery:** SDR before approach (assume surveillance). Non-obvious approach. Retrieve, no sign, different exit.

**Degradation:** Food (1–6 months), batteries (1 year), meds (variable), water (indefinite sealed). Rotate perishables.
