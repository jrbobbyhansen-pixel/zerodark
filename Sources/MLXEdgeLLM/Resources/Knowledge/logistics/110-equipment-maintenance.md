# Equipment Maintenance in the Field

**Philosophy:** Fix breaking before broken. Inspect before use. Clean after use. Don't wait field failure.

**Weapons:** After use – clear, field strip, barrel clean (patches until clean), lightly lube (bolt carrier, rails, trigger), reassemble, function check. Water immersion – immediate disassembly, dry, lube. Sand/dust – immediate clean. Sparse lube sandy (collects sand).

**Communications:** Batteries – charge before op, carry spare. Degrade 20–30% cold (keep body heat). Inspect antennas. Clean contacts pencil eraser if intermittent.

**Optics:** Clean cloth only. No solvents. Caps when not using. Zero-check after impact.

**Night vision:** Never expose powered to direct light. Store caps on. Batteries – lithium better cold than alkaline.

**First aid:** Inventory after use. Replace expended before next op. Check dates quarterly.

**Personal:** Footwear – dry thoroughly. Waterproof reapply (beeswax, commercial). Inspect soles. Clothing – repair tears early. Sleep system – dry store (moisture degrades).
