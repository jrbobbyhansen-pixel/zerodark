# Ammunition Management and Conservation

**Count/track:** Before and after contact. Brief status after. Establish threshold – below = report, change tactics, request resupply.

**Conservation:** Aimed only – auto emergency only (20x consumption semi). Identify targets. Assess effect before continuing. Suppress, don't destroy.

**Resupply priority:** Weapons before anything. Order: primary weapon, secondary, less-lethal.

**Distribution:** Equalize after contact. One high expender? Redistribute from high counts. Team capability = total / members.

**Emergency load:** Min combat effective 3–5 magazines (90–150 rounds rifle). Below = request resupply, preserve.

**Compatibility:** All weapons same caliber cross-feed capable. 5.56 vs 7.62 NOT compatible. Brief team.

**Captured:** Only if chamber/caliber identical. Casing markings: caliber, manufacturer. Foreign may be corrosive (increase cleaning).
