# Load Planning and Rucksack Packing

**Weight:** Optimal fighting = 33% body weight. 30% sustained. >50% significant degradation. 170lb = 56lbs max fight load.

**Load categories:** Fighting – weapon, ammo, water, first aid, comms (what you fight). Approach march – above + mission essentials (food, sleep, extra water). Administrative – left at vehicles/base.

**Packing:** Heavy items – close back, high (CoG over hips). Medium – main accessible. Light/infrequent – bottom, outer. Immediate access – top, side pockets (first aid, water, comms).

**Never cut:** Water – min 2L body. First aid – tourniquet accessible. Comms – charged. Navigation – map, compass, phone.

**Duration contents:** 12hr – 2–4L water, 2 meals, first aid, ammo, comms. 24hr – add 6L water, 3 meals, poncho, battery. 72hr – add 9L capacity, 9 meals, sleep system, gear.

**Reduce weight:** Remove duplicates. Cut food packaging. Consolidate first aid. Multi-use items. Accept comfort loss before safety.
