# Power Management – Batteries and Alternative Sources

**Types:** Lithium – cold performance (−20°F), 10yr shelf, non-rechargeable, expensive. Best emergency/cold. Lithium-ion rechargeable – energy dense, degrade age/deep discharge, temp sensitive. Alkaline – moderate temps acceptable, −32°F significant loss, available.

**Cold:** Alkaline 40–50% loss at 0°F. Lithium 10–15%. Keep warm (inside clothing, pocket). Warm alkaline recovers 50–75%.

**Power budget:** Inventory devices. Estimate daily use. Calculate capacity. Prioritize: comms, navigation, lights, other.

**Solar:** 100W panel charges USB, 12V. Efficient right angle 10:00–14:00. Cloudy 20–30% rated. Zero shade/indirect.

**Hand crank:** Emergency only (inefficient). 10 min crank = 1–2 min call. Reserve emergencies. Fatiguing sustained.

**Reduction:** Screen brightness (max impact, reduce). GPS demand-only. Apps close. Airplane mode unused comms.

**Standardization:** Team same battery types (logistics). Same form factor. USB-C one cable all.
