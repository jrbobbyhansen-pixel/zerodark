# Water Purification – Making Any Water Safe

**Boiling (VERIFIED most reliable):** Rolling boil 1 full minute at sea level. 3 minutes above 6,500ft. Kills all biological threats including Giardia, Cryptosporidium, bacteria, viruses.

## Improvised Filter (Pre-Treatment Only, Must Boil After)

Layer from bottom to top in container: coarse grass/cloth – fine sand – crushed charcoal – gravel. Run water through twice. Removes particles and improves taste. Does NOT kill pathogens.

## Chemical Treatment

8 drops unscented household bleach (6% sodium hypochlorite) per 1 gallon clear water. Double to 16 drops for cloudy water. Wait 30 minutes. Water should smell faintly of chlorine.

## SODIS UV Method

Fill clear plastic bottle, lay flat in direct sun 6+ hours (2 full days if cloudy). Effective above 50°F. Does not work in turbid water.

## Signs Water is Unsafe Regardless of Method

Chemical smell, foam, iridescent sheen (oil contamination), upstream industrial or agricultural activity, dead animals near source.

## Never Treat and Drink

Seawater, chemical-contaminated water (industrial runoff), heavy-metal contaminated water (old mining areas).
