# Drone Operations – Use and Defense

**Commercial capability:** Optical – 4K, 30x zoom, recognizable 200m. Thermal – heat through moderate vegetation. Range – 5–10km consumer. Flight – 20–40 min/battery.

**Military indicators:** Persistent orbit = surveillance. Back-forth = search. Low (<50m) = following target. Fixed hover = watching.

**Counter-detection:** Acoustic – audible 100–200m quiet. High-pitched buzz 75–125Hz. Optical – daylight visible. RF – 2.4GHz or 5.8GHz. SDR detects.

**Counter-denial:** Overhead cover (most effective) – trees, bridges, structures. Thermal suppression – space blanket, emergency blanket under camo. Slow movement under concealment. RF jamming – requires equipment, illegal without auth.

**ZeroDark use:** LiDAR scan route recon before movement. Map for OP selection. Document terrain. Below treeline when possible. Minimize flight time.
