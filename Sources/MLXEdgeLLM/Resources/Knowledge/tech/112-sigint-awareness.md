# SIGINT Awareness – Signals Intelligence

**Collected:** Electronic (radio, WiFi, BT, cellular). Location (cell tower triangulation, photo GPS metadata). Pattern (call records, message metadata, history). Content (unencrypted).

**RDF:** Directional antenna bearing to transmission. 2+ stations triangulate location. Accuracy 10–50m modern. Time 2–5 min professional team. Counter: minimize transmission, change location after, low power, indirect.

**IMSI catchers (Stingrays):** Mimic tower, phones connect, collect IDs (IMEI, IMSI) and sometimes content. Detect: unusual battery drain, signal anomalies. Counter: airplane mode, Faraday pouch.

**Metadata:** Who, when, where, how long – even without content. Builds pattern of life. Encrypted content still produces metadata.

**Photo EXIF:** Embeds GPS, time, camera default. Strip before sharing operational photos.

**Countermeasures:** Operational phones separate. Airplane mode sensitive areas. Faraday bags. Battery removal possible. Vary patterns. Encrypted messaging (Signal). Strip metadata files.
