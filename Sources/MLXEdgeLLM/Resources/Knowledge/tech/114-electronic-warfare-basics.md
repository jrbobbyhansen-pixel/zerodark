# Electronic Warfare Fundamentals

**Categories:** EA – jamming, spoofing, EMP. EP – your protection measures. ES – collect/analyze enemy.

**GPS jamming:** Noise same frequency (1.575 GHz L1) overwhelms signal. Result – loss lock, errors, false position. Detect – sudden loss no natural cause. Counter – dead reckoning, MGRS map, terrain association.

**GPS spoofing:** False signals broadcast, incorrect position. Against vehicles, drones, systems. More sophisticated than jamming. Detect – position inconsistent terrain.

**Radio jamming:** Broadband noise overwhelms frequency. Can't transmit/receive. Detect – sudden comms loss area. Counter – frequency agility (PACE alternates), other methods (visual, Meshtastic LoRa different band).

**EMP:** High-altitude nuclear – fries unshielded electronics large area. Faraday cage protects. Hardened – military-grade designed survive. Practical – critical electronics (radio, meds) metal container (ammo can) improvised cage.
