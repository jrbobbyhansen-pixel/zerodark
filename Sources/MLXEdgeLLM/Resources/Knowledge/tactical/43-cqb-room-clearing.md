# CQB Room Clearing – Fundamentals

**Fatal funnel:** The doorway. Bullets, fragmentation, and observation concentrate here. Never pause in a doorway. Move through with commitment and speed.

**Pre-entry:**
Slice the pie from outside doorway: stand at door edge, step incrementally outward while leaning inward – gain angle on as much of room as possible before committing to entry. Goal: see threat before entering.

**Two-person entry:**
#1 takes near side, #2 takes far side.
Buttonhook: #1 peels immediately to nearest wall on entry.
Cross: #2 crosses to far corner diagonally – clears maximum room fastest, highest exposure.
#1 calls "near corner clear." #2 calls "far corner clear." Together: "room clear."

**Movement:**
Move muzzle first, then body. Clear corners with weapon before exposing body. Travel along walls, not through center. Never cross partner's line of fire.

**Threat prioritization:**
Nearest threat first. Weapon-bearing threats before unarmed. Mobile threats before stationary.

**Stairwells:**
Most dangerous confined space. Clear from maximum distance – extend pie as far as possible before committing to stairwell. Clear landing above before advancing. Never rush stairwells.

**Multiple rooms:**
Establish control of cleared rooms before advancing. Post security facing uncleared areas. Move in series, not simultaneously through multiple breaches.

**Common errors:**
Pausing in fatal funnel. Crossing ahead of partner. Muzzle sweeping friendlies. Premature "clear" call before full room cleared. All fatal errors.
