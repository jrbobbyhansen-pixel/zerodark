# React to Contact – Immediate Action Drills

**Near ambush (within 50m):**
ASSAULT THROUGH immediately. Do not stay in kill zone – movement is survival. Lay down maximum fire and close with ambush element. Simultaneous: shout "CONTACT LEFT/RIGHT/FRONT," fire toward threat, assault toward them. Speed and violence of action disrupts ambush.

**Far ambush (beyond 50m):**
BREAK CONTACT. Immediate suppression on contact point. Bounding withdrawal: half the element suppresses while other half moves rearward. Alternate until out of direct fire. Cover each bound.

**Contact drills sequence:**
1. Return fire immediately – volume suppresses even if inaccurate
2. "CONTACT [direction] [distance]" shout
3. Take cover – even partial cover is better than standing
4. Assess – is this a near or far ambush?
5. React accordingly – assault or break contact
6. Consolidate – account for all personnel
7. Report up

**Right peel (break contact):**
Element in line. Rightmost person fires and peels backward past the formation. Next rightmost follows. Creates continuous fire while everyone withdraws in turn.

**Actions on IED/command-detonated device:**
Immediate: Off road if possible. Stop. Security 360°. Assess for secondary device or follow-on ambush. Treat casualties. Report.
