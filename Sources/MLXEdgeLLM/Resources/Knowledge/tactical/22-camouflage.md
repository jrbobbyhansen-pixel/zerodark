# Advanced Camouflage

## Shape Disruption

Humans recognize human body shape. Break it with irregular attachments — vegetation, netting, irregular fabric strips.

## Shine Elimination

Tape or paint all exposed metal. Cover or remove glass lenses. Remove all reflective materials.

## Shadow Use

Move between shadows. Pause in shadow to observe. Never silhouette against sky or light background.

## Natural Vegetation Attachment

Use locally present species. Replace when wilting (every 2-4 hours). Never cut vegetation from immediately around OP (creates obvious gaps). Match density and species.

## Face Paint

Three colors — dominant base matching terrain, secondary disruption breaking facial features (nose/chin/forehead highlight areas), dark around eyes.

## Thermal Signature Reduction

Reflective space blanket under ghillie reduces IR signature. Stay in shade during day (reduces contrast). Avoid proximity to heat sources.
