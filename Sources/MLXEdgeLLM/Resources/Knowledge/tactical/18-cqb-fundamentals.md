# CQB Fundamentals — Close Quarters Battle Basics

**Fatal funnel:** The doorway concentrates fire. Never stand in a doorway. Move through quickly with purpose.

## Entry Techniques

### Buttonhook
Peel to nearest wall after threshold, clear near corner.

### Cross
Go to far wall, clears more of room, more exposure during crossing.

## Two-Person Room Clearance

#1 enters and buttonhooks to near corner. #2 enters and crosses to far corner. Both call "clear" only when entire room confirmed empty. Communicate before entry: signal (tap on shoulder), count, go.

## Stairwells

Most dangerous confined space. Slice the pie from distance: gain angle on each step from maximum distance before committing. Never rush up. Clear bottom-up with overwatch.

## Trigger Discipline

Finger outside trigger until on target with decision made to fire. In confined spaces with friendly personnel this rule prevents fratricide.

## Weapons Handling

Move muzzle before moving feet. Clear corners with weapon first, then body. Minimize exposure — use only what is necessary to see and engage threat.
