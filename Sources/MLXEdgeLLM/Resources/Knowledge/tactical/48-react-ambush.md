# React to Ambush – Drill

When you are the ambushed element, immediate action saves lives. Hesitation kills.

**Near ambush (under 50m – in the kill zone):**
1. IMMEDIATELY return fire – volume, not accuracy, initially
2. Simultaneously move – assault INTO the ambush
3. Shout "CONTACT [direction]" as you're already moving and shooting
4. Assault through – close with and destroy the ambush
5. Reorganize beyond the kill zone on a natural rally point

Rationale: Staying in the kill zone = death. Moving = disrupting fire control, closing distance where ambushers have less advantage.

**Far ambush (over 50m):**
1. IMMEDIATELY return fire (suppress)
2. Move to nearest cover
3. Assess and control
4. Suppress and bound rearward
5. Break contact using right peel or bounding overwatch

**Immediate response (any ambush, first 3 seconds):**
Everyone fires immediately. Fire at muzzle flashes, tracers, or direction of rounds. Move simultaneously. Don't wait for orders.

**Casevac during contact:**
Drag casualties to cover. Do NOT stop and treat in kill zone. Continue fight, stabilize when in cover.

**Likely ambush indicators:**
Unnatural silence. Animals flushing suddenly. Tripwire vegetation. Unusual objects on trail. "Gut feeling" – trust it, change route.
