# Counter-Surveillance

## Foot Surveillance Indicators

Same person in two or more separate locations. Person mirrors your direction changes. Phone use repeatedly while moving. Person positioned at choke points.

## Three-Contact Rule

Same person, three locations = surveillance confirmed.

## Surveillance Detection Route (SDR)

Plan route with 2-3 choke points (narrow alleys, doorways, stairwells) where surveillance must commit and expose themselves. Include reversals and illogical direction changes.

## Losing Surveillance

Natural reversals through stores with multiple exits. Mode changes (enter subway, exit immediately, enter taxi). Crowd merging with appearance change.

## Electronic Surveillance

Cell phones broadcast location to towers continuously even without GPS. Apps request location constantly. Bluetooth and WiFi passively scan and transmit unique device IDs every 2-10 seconds. IMSI catchers collect device identifiers from up to 1 mile away.

## Counter-Electronic

Airplane mode or Faraday pouch when sensitive. Dedicated phones per operation.
