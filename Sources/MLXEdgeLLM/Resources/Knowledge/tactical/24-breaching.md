# Forced Entry

## Mechanical

### Halligan Bar
Fork end under hinge or beside lock, strike with mallet, lever to break.

### Bolt Cutters
Padlock shackle at body junction (not center of shackle).

### Battering Ram
Strike beside the lock, not center of door.

## Lock Picking (Improvised)

Tension wrench (flat metal strip, flathead screwdriver). Pick (bobby pin bent at tip). Apply very light rotational tension. Lift each pin individually until it sets. Rotate when all pins set.

## Vehicle Entry

Rear quarter approach (avoids engine block and driver sightlines). Pop rear window via rubber mallet around seal perimeter. Slim jim down between door and window, hook the linkage.

## Window

Strike corner of glass with sharp object (weakest point). Remove glass from frame before entry.
