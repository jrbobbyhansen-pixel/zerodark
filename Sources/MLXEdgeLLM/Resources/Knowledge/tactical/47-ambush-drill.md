# Ambush Drill

**Linear ambush:**
Element in line parallel to expected enemy route. Lead element allows enemy to fully enter kill zone before initiating. Initiation: most devastating weapon (claymore equivalent or most accurate shooter) fires first – all others immediately follow. Assault through or withdraw depending on mission.

**L-shaped ambush:**
Main element along expected route. Short element perpendicular at far end of kill zone. Short element engages to prevent escape, blocks withdrawal. Main element fires along route.

**Setup:**
Establish security teams 50–100m from kill zone on both flanks. Claymores (or equivalent) prepared. Everyone briefed on initiation signal, sector of fire, and withdrawal route.

**Kill zone:**
Selected terrain that canalize enemy, limits their cover, and maximizes exposure to fire. Bend in road/trail, bridge, chokepoint.

**Actions after ambush:**
Assault through (offensive): sweep kill zone, collect intelligence, destroy equipment. Withdraw to rally point.
Break contact (defensive): suppress, withdraw by bounds to rally point.
Either way: account for all personnel before departing area.

**Reconnaissance before:**
Walk the kill zone before occupation. Note terrain features. Assign sectors. Establish signal.
