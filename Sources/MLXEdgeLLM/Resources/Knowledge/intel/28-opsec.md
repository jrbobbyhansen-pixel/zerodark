# Operational Security (OPSEC)

## Five-Step OPSEC Process

1. Identify critical information (what would adversary want to know?)
2. Analyze threats (who wants it, what resources do they have?)
3. Analyze vulnerabilities (how could they find out?)
4. Assess risk (likelihood × impact)
5. Apply countermeasures

## Electronic Emissions

Cell phones broadcast location to towers continuously even without GPS. Apps request location constantly. Bluetooth and WiFi passively scan and transmit unique device IDs every 2-10 seconds. IMSI catchers collect device identifiers from up to 1 mile.

## Communications Security

Brevity codes (pre-agreed innocuous phrases). Frequency changes on schedule. Authentication codes (challenge/password pairs). Never use real names or locations on unsecured channels.

## Physical Security

Document destruction (burn or shred — folding and tearing is insufficient). Counter-surveillance before sensitive meetings. Need-to-know strictly enforced.
