# SALUTE Reporting Format

SALUTE is the standard format for reporting enemy/threat activity.

**S – Size:**
Number of personnel. Number and type of vehicles. Number of aircraft. Estimate if exact unknown: "approximately 15 personnel," "1 vehicle estimated."

**A – Activity:**
What are they doing right now? Moving, stationary, emplacing equipment, conducting surveillance, loading/unloading vehicles, digging.

**L – Location:**
MGRS 8–10 digit grid. Or: description relative to known point. "10-digit MGRS: 14SNA0645028563." Or: "200m east of highway 87, north side of road."

**U – Unit:**
Any identifying information: uniforms, insignia, vehicle markings, equipment signatures, call signs heard, language spoken. "Unknown – civilian clothing, no markings."

**T – Time:**
Time the activity was OBSERVED, not reported. "Observed 1347 local." Use 24-hour format.

**E – Equipment:**
Weapons visible (type if identifiable). Communications equipment. Vehicles (type, color, markings). Special equipment (optical, medical, engineering).

**Example:**
"SALUTE follows: Size – 6 personnel. Activity – Moving east on foot in file formation. Location – Grid 14SNA064285. Unit – Unknown, civilian clothing, no markings. Time – 1423 local. Equipment – 3 carrying long weapons, 1 with backpack radio. Break. All personnel departed east, last seen moving toward treeline at grid 14SNA071285. End."
