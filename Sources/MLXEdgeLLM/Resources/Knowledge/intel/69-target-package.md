# Target Package Development

**Purpose:** A target package provides all information needed to engage, surveil, capture, or otherwise act on a specific target – person, vehicle, facility, or activity.

**Components:**

**Target Identification:**
Name/alias or description. Physical description (height, weight, age, distinguishing features). Photograph if available. Known associates.

**Location data:**
Known addresses (primary, alternate, workplace). Routes of travel. Frequency of locations. Grid coordinates of significant locations.

**Activity pattern:**
Schedule (if known). Pattern of life summary. Vehicles used (description, registration). Communications methods used.

**Threat assessment:**
Known security measures. Armed? What weapons? Known associates who provide security? Awareness level.

**Collection requirements:**
What information is still needed? What collection asset can provide it?

**Restrictions:**
Rules of engagement applicable. Prohibited actions. Legal authorities required.

**Execution:**
Recommended action (surveillance, apprehension, etc.). Recommended timing and location. Resources required. Alternate plans.

**Source handling:**
All information sourced with reliability code. Unverified information clearly marked. Conflicting information noted and analyzed.
