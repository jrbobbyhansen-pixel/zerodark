# Improvised Medical Resources

## Wound Irrigation

Plastic bag with small hole or syringe. 300-600mL clean water under pressure. Removes contamination better than dabbing.

## Improvised Sutures

Fishing line (20lb+), dental floss, fine thread. Simple interrupted pattern: 4-5mm apart, 4mm from edge. Surgeon's knot.

## Natural Antimicrobials

Honey applied directly to wounds (antimicrobial, reduces infection – widely cited in wound care literature). Garlic juice. Propolis.

## Improvised Saline

1/4 tsp non-iodized salt per liter of boiled water. For wound irrigation only.

## Pain

Willow bark tea (contains salicylates, aspirin precursor – steep bark in boiling water 20 minutes). Positioning and distraction significantly reduce perceived pain.
