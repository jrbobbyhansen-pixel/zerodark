# Shock – Recognition and Treatment

Signs: Pale/cool/clammy skin. Rapid weak pulse (over 100bpm). Rapid breathing (over 20/min). Confusion. Thirst. Anxiety.

## Treatment

1. Stop the bleeding first
2. Lay flat on back
3. Elevate legs 12in (UNLESS head/spine injury or breathing difficulty)
4. Prevent heat loss – cover with anything available
5. Nothing by mouth
6. Calm reassurance

## Do NOT

Give alcohol. Leave patient alone.

## Improvement Signs

Skin warms, pulse slows, mental clarity returns.
