# Airway Management

## Unconscious Patient

Tilt head back and lift chin. Pulls tongue off posterior pharynx. Suspected spine injury: jaw thrust only (push mandible forward without moving neck).

## Recovery Position

On side, top knee forward, arm under head. Prevents choking on vomit.

## Choking Conscious

5 back blows between shoulder blades with heel of hand, then 5 abdominal thrusts (stand behind, fist above navel, sharp inward-upward). Alternate until resolved or patient loses consciousness.

## CPR

Heel of hand on center of chest. Compress 2 inches at 100 beats per minute. 30 compressions + 2 breaths. Hands-only if untrained. Do not stop until patient breathes, help arrives, or physical exhaustion.
