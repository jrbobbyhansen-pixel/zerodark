# Burns, Hypothermia, Heat Stroke

## Burns

Cool running water 20 full minutes within 3 hours. Do NOT: ice, butter, toothpaste. Cover with clean non-stick dressing. Third-degree: white/leathery/painless – cover and evacuate. Airway burns (singed facial hair, hoarse voice, soot in nostrils): priority evacuation – airway can swell shut in hours.

## Hypothermia

Shivering = mild. Stops shivering + confusion = severe.
Treatment: Remove wet clothing (wet = 25× faster heat loss). Insulate from ground. Warm core first (armpits/groin/neck). Warm sweet fluids if conscious. Do NOT rub extremities.

## Heat Stroke

Hot DRY skin + confusion + no sweating = emergency.
Immerse in cold water. Fan aggressively. Cool neck/armpits/groin. Target 1°F drop per 5 minutes.
